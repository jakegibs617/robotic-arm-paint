# Marker holder mount (Phase 4)

A 3D-printed holder that replaces/ignores the gripper and clamps a Sharpie or
Posca marker to the wrist plate.

## Requirements

- Holds a Sharpie or Posca marker rigidly.
- Mounts to the wrist plate (match the kit's bolt pattern — measure during Phase 1).
- Allows marker tip-height adjustment.
- Does not collide with the arm through its range of motion.
- Prefer slight compliance (foam pad, light spring, or flexible clamp) so the tip
  keeps contact pressure without rigidly fighting small Z errors.
